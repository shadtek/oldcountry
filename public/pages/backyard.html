Backyard BBQ Smokers

We have two styles of back yard BBQ smokers, the All American BBQ Smokers and the Old Country BBQ Pits. They both produce great tasting barbeque with less effort than ordinary BBQ Smokers.
Our BBQ Smokers are designed to reduce hot and cold spots within the cooking chamber, to retain the heat, prevent smoke from leaking, have a long life and, basically, aid you to barbeque.

All American BBQ Pits

They have an excellent heat retention capacity. They have a big firebox baffle and the smoke stack is placed in the center of the grill section this helps to reduce the cold and hot spots in the grill section. The fire box has a fire grate designed to promote air flow and sliding cooking grate.

Old Country BBQ Smokers

These are the old style BBQ smokers they are built with 1/4" thick steel plate and are completely welded. Even though this is the classic style it has some new features like the expanded metal grates which are framed with angle iron and slide in and out of the BBQ smoker. They also have stainless steel "stay cool" spring handles, adjustable air intake, and a folding table. The vertical chamber has 3 grates, 2 rod hangers and a temperature gauge port, it is also flat in the bottom for the people who like to place a water bowl. The fire box has a fire grate designed to promote air flow and sliding cooking grate. They also have an excellent at heat retention and will help you BBQ.

Old Country BBQ Pits

These classic style bbq pits are built with 1/4" thick steel plate. They have an all welded construction. They have an excellent heat retention capacity. They have a big firebox baffle and the smoke stack is placed in the center of the grill section this helps to reduce the cold and hot spots in the grill section. They come with framed slide out grills, stainless steel spring handles, folding table, adjustable air intake and adjustable smoke stack. The fire box has a fire grate designed to promote air flow and sliding cooking grate.

Outdoor Fire Pits

For many, many years people in Texas and Northern Mexico have gathered around camp fires. Whether they are camping, hunting, fishing or spending time with friends and family, there is something special about a camp fire that bonds people like few other things can.

Traditionally the instrument of choice for a safe campfire is the outdoor fire pit also known as an outdoor fireplace. They have come in many shapes and sizes. Today one of the most popular types is the Old Country style of outdoor fire pits. There are many companies selling this style of fire pits and with good reason. This is a proven design that works.

The Old Country Outdoor Fire Pits (or outdoor fireplaces) currently come in two styles; the Extended Grill Fire pit and the Double Grill fire pit.

Both pits have the following features in common:

label Leg extensions to bring them to waist height.

label Expanded metal grill for barbequing

label Angle Iron frame for the expanded metal grill

label Swivel grill

label Stainless steel handle

label Minimum of 3/16" thick steel

label Hand Made

There is another feature these fire pits share: Compared with other fire pits, the Old Country pits give you the biggest bang for the buck.

The Extended Grill Outdoor Fire Pit

This fire pit comes with a ring of expanded metal around it, it works great for setting stuff on it or to rest and warm your boots any cold night.

The Double Grill Outdoor Fire Pit

This fire pit has a second grill on it. It is half the the regular grill and can be used to warm your tortillas or keep anything from getting cold.
