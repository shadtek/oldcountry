Backyard BBQ Grills

We have two styles of back yard BBQ grills, All American and Old Country. They both produce great tasting barbeque with less effort than ordinary BBQ grills.

Questions regarding the BBQ grills? contact us!

All American BBQ Grills

This BBQ grill is made of 3/16" steel plate and all welded together.
It comes with a fire grate, a framed slide out cooking grill and an extra upper sliding grill. It also has the smoke stack placed in the center for better a heat distribution. With its wheels and casters it is easy to move and with the folding table it uses less space for storage. If what you want to do is BBQ grill this is an excellent piece of equipment.

Old Country BBQ Grills

This BBQ grill is made of 1/4" steel plate and all welded together. It comes with a fire grate, a framed slide out cooking grill and an extra upper sliding grill. It also has the smoke stack placed in the center for better a heat distribution. It has 2 big steel wheels that make it easy to move on uneven surfaces and with the folding table it uses less space for storage.
If what you want to do is BBQ grill this is the best equipment.

Old Country Camper BBQ Grills

These BBQ grills are great units, they fit almost anywhere are made with 1/4" thick steel and cook great. Also they can be attached to your pick up hitch for tail gating.
