Insulated Portable BBQ Smokers

This is the upgraded model of our portable BBQ Smokers.
The first thing you will notice is the rectangular grill section as opposed to the cylindrical grill section.
Most Portable BBQ Smoker fabricators use pipe to build their smokers therefore their smokers have to be round.
Because of our fabricating methods, we can look for the shape that best accommodates the bbquer's needs.

Some of the features of this upgraded BBQ Smoker are the following:

label It comes in either FULLY INSULATED or single wall option.

label It is built using all new materials and 1/4" steel plate (or thicker)

label Rectangular BBQ Smokers are bigger than cylindrical BBQ Smokers.

label All the cooking grates have the same width.

label Bigger grate area.

label The grill section is taller in the rectangular unit than in the cylindrical unit.

label The lids open left and right instead of having to lift them

label The top of the grill section can be used as a huge warm (or hot) surface
